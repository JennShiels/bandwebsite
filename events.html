The Drop Outs

Coming to a town near you!

We are a two-piece band from County Galway. Rebecca hails from Ballina in Mayo and is the female vocalist of the band while Rory plays guitar and is the male vocalist in the band. The pair met back in 2015 when Rebecca joined a wedding band in which Rory had playing with for over a year as lead guitarist and male singer. In 2017 they went their separate ways but soon formed The Dropouts Galway. As the new set up, they started playing in all sorts of venues; including pubs and nightclubs. With future plans to expand into the wedding scene once more The Dropouts Galway offer a truely pesonalised sound and are the life and soul of any event entertainment.
