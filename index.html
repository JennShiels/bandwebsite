Male Singer and Guitarist.

Rory on Twitter

Rory is a singer and self taught guitarist from Galway. Rory started playing guitar at the age of eighteen. After discovering such artists as Tom Petty, Bob Dillon and Bruce Springsteen, Rory was inspired and felt the disire to re-create the sounds for himself. Some years later Rory joined a wedding band called The Loveshack Band and travelled around Ireland performing at Weddings and in nightclubs. Today Rory is part of a duet called The Dropouts who play in various venues around Galway County.

Female Singer and Kahone player

Becks on Twitter

Becks is the main localist of the duo and plays a damn good beat on the kahone. Becks is a singer from Mayo. Becks started performing at a very young age and is a classically trained singer. After discovering such artists as Tom Petty, Bob Dillon and Bruce Springsteen, Becks joined The Loveshack Band a year after Rory which is how they met. Today Becks is part of The Dropouts taking lead on vocals.
